Movie Recommendations | October 2021

The Departed

Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Jack Nicholson, Matt Damon, Mark Wahlberg, Martin Sheen, Vera Farmiga, Alec Baldwin
Released Year: 2006
 
My rating - 9/10
IMdB- 8.6/10
Rotten Tomatoes- Critics 90% | Audience 94%
