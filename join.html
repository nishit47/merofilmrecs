About CiNepal

CiNepal is a local club of Nepali film enthusiasts and filmmakers, where we discuss movies and the craft of making them.
 
Objectives:
  • Discuss and recommend movies
  • Provide a platform to critique, review and recommend movies
  • Create a strong local filmmaking network
  • Increase film literacy
  • Promote local movies
 
Who can join?
Anyone with a love for movies.
