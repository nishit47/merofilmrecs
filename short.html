Short Movies You Can Watch On Youtube

Aahar

"Aahar" explores a small family somewhere in Nepal which is deeply affected by Covid-19 and how it deals with it.
 
 
Director: Swayam Aryal
Cast: Aagaman Karki, Sanzu Kuikel, Aayan Aryal, Hiroshi Khanal, Siddhartha Nepal
Released Year: 2021
 
My rating - 8/10
 
 
 

Sambandhak

SAMBANDHAK is the story of Neha and her two brothers Ramesh and Raju. Things get complicated when Neha gets the opportunity to go abroad to pursue her dream of being a graphic designer but she has to leave her family who depend on her more than she knows.
 
 
Director: Siddhartha Pudasaini
Cast: Simanta Pudasaini, Mira Khadka, Sandesh Shakya, Aakanshya G.C
Released Year: 2020
 
My rating - 8/10
